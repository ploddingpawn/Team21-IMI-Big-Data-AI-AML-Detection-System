Stage 03 — Three-Pillar Hybrid Architecture
Pillar I

Typology-Specific Isolation Forests

Five independent Isolation Forest models — each tuned to a distinct AML typology (Structuring, Layering, Rapid Movement, Profile Mismatch, High-Risk Geography). Per-model anomaly scores are fed into fusion weighted by feature coverage: sparse profiles fall back to IF-only scores.

Pillar II

Deterministic Rule-Based Flags

Vectorized hard checks enforce regulatory thresholds and industry limits. Each triggered rule contributes a quantified signal. Rules are mapped to indicator IDs (e.g., STRUCT-003, ATYPICAL-007) which propagate into the final explanation.

Pillar III

K-Means Peer-Group Clustering

K-Means clusters customers into behaviorally similar peer groups on the normalized IF score space. Cluster tier becomes a secondary risk signal — validated via silhouette score — contextualising outliers within their peer cohort before final score normalization.
